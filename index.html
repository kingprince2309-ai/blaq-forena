About Blaq Forena

Our story
JD

Justice Kwaku Duah, Chief Executive Officer

Blaq Forena was founded in Accra, Ghana in 2021 as a general trading business and has grown into a multi-sector company spanning commodities, gold trading, borehole construction, logistics, and biomass exports. The company operates from Adenta, Greater Accra, and has expanded its registered activities over time to include agro-produce cultivation and processing, civil engineering works, and import/export services.

Every division shares the same foundation: reliable, well-documented delivery for partners who need a dependable Ghanaian trade and logistics partner.
